Our Story

The owner is a Canadian expat who arrived in Kaohsiung in August of 2000.
Gareth has lived and taught in Taiwan for over 19 years.
In 2010, The Magic Spoon was founded to provide locally made premium ice cream.
In August 2017, The Magic Spoon Brunch House was officially opened.
Our goal is to provide a high quality, homemade brunch experience that cannot be replicated at home.
We source most of our ingredients locally and through fellow expats who are also producing top notch ingredients which we use in our brunches.
We hope that you enjoy your time with us and if you have ANY questions, comments or concerns, we encourage you to share them with either Gareth or any of the competent staff here.
The Magic Spoon Brunch House... Are you up for it?

Our Promise

The Magic Spoon Brunch House strives for an extremely high level of quality and freshness.
At The Magic Spoon Brunch House we only use organic eggs, flour, whole milk, imported cream, whole fruit and high quality meat and vegetables.
Our sausage meat is locally sourced through Cory's Kitchen our hot sauces are made by another Canadian at Greenspoons's.
If you love us tell your friends. If your experience here did not meet expectations, please bring it to our attention.
We aim to make your time here memorable(for all the right reasons).
